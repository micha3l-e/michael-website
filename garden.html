Thoughts On Spirals

Winding, unwinding, and so on...

Winding around a central point, the spiral can appear to both ascend and descend simultaneously. This has become indicative of two opposing, and sometimes contradictory experiences. One in which there is an introspective, and recursive inward screw into dreadful anxiety; and another in which there is an outward winding of joyous wonderment.
