Thoughts On Spirals

winding, unwinding, and so on

Winding around a central point, the spiral can appear to both ascend and descend simultaneously. Two opposing, and sometimes contradictory experiences. One in which there is an introspective, and recursive inward screw into dreadful anxiety; and another in which there is an outward unwinding of joyous wonderment.
